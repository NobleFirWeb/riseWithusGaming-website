Zack Alvarez is a champion-ranked gamer and content creator from Burley, Idaho, competing on PS5 across Madden, Forza, and Fortnite. He started streaming in December 2025 and quickly discovered what makes it real — the people.

"I quickly fell in love with the community, friendly competition, and the connections gaming creates."

For Zack, streaming is more than entertainment — it keeps his mental health positive while doing what he loves most: gaming, meeting new people, and having fun with the squad. That's the energy he brings to Rise With Us Gaming every time he goes live.
