About DTB

More Than
A Game

Gaming has always been bigger than just entertainment for DTB. His father taught him how to play Madden and always pushed him to start streaming — not just to compete, but to build something real.

"After he passed away on October 9, 2024, I decided to turn that motivation into something meaningful and continue the passion we shared together."

— DTB

Rise With Us Gaming is the result of that promise. Built from grief, fueled by love, and driven by a commitment to compete and create at the highest level — every stream, every match, is dedicated to the man who started it all.
